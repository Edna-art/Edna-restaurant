About Edna's Gourmet Restaurant


Brasilian tradition from Minas Gerais, opened since 2013

Just before Christmas 2013, Edna's Gourmet Restaurant opened it's door in a prime location in New York City. Located in midtown Manhattan, our first restaurant in the Big Apple is a short walk from Times Square and just steps away from MoMA. The culinary team has continued the tradition of developing new menu creations rooted in the Brazilian tradition that push forward the Brazilian culinary experience. In 2015, the Bar Edna's Gourmet introduced guests to new Brazilian-inspired small plates, award-winning wines and cocktails.
